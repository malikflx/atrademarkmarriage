Meet Mr. & Mrs. Felix

As Brooklyn, NY natives, we have had stirring upbringings. We've had the opportunity to live in different parts of New York City and even different states which allowed us to see life from several perspectives, lifestyles, and cultures, which have given us a unique view of society. We have a massive love for music (especially live music), sing and play instruments. We're also parents who greatly value family. Your well-being and the value you can bring into this world is important to us. Standing upon our spiritual foundation, we decided to pursue a new initiative aimed at breaking the generational curses forced upon many communities. Through Honest, Open, and Transparent (H.O.T!) conversations about our marriage, we hope to "break the rules" of popular culture and create a safe space to have hard conversations that influence actions for a widespread change!
